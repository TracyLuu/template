Allied Moulded Enclosures

For more than half-a-century, Allied Moulded Products has been supplying non-metallic enclosures for a variety of control applications such as marine, SCADA, security, solar, telecommunications, wastewater treatment, and wind turbines. Their product line of NEMA 4X/IEC Type IP66 fiberglass and polycarbonate enclosures provide maximum protection against water, salt, chemicals, and UV.

  • Fiberglass
  • Polycarbonate

FiberglassEnclosures

Enclosures range in size from small junction boxes up to large free-standing enclosures. They are molded with Allied Mouldeds proprietary ULTRAGUARD (FRP) formulation which resists the effects of UV degradation. Ideal for both indoor/outdoor applications and high temperature or corrosive environments.

Features
  • Nonmetallic and stainless steel locking hardware
  • Available in clear, windowed, and solid covers
  • Stainless steel hardware
  • Sizes from 7 x 4 x 3 up through 72 x 49 x 25

PolycarbonateEnclosures

The POLYLINE AMP Series are an aesthetically pleasing and durable alternative to fiberglass enclosures. Lightweight, easy-to-machine, and cost-effective enclosure solution designed for indoor and outdoor applications.

Features
  • Molded-in DIN Rail bosses
  • Wall mounting flanges and feet
  • Nonmetallic and stainless steel latches
  • Available in clear and solid covers
  • Stainless steel hardware
  • Sizes from 4 x 4 x 3 up to 18 x 16 x 10
